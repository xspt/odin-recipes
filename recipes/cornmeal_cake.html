Cornmeal cake

Description

A traditional brazilian recipe, this simple cake is perfect for the breakfast along a cup of coffee

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 1 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups of cornmeal
  • 1 cup of milk

Steps

  1. Beat the sugar, milk, eggs and oil in a blender
  2. Add the flour and the cornmeal and beat again
  3. Add the baking powder and beat just until mixed
  4. Take to the preheated oven at 180ÂºC for around 45 minutes
