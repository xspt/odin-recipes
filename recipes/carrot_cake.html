Carrot cake


Description

A delicious and simple cake made with carrots, perfect for breakfast or coffee break

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 1/2 cup of flour
  • 3 medium grated carrots
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder

Chocolate glaze

  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 3 tablespoons of chocolate powder
  • 1 cup of milk

Steps

Dough

  1. Inside a blender, add the carrots, eggs and oil, then beat
  2. Add sugar and beat again for around 5 minutes
  3. In a bowl, pour the dough and add the flour, mix again
  4. Add the baking powder and mix slowly
  5. Bake in preheated oven at 180Â°C for around 40 minutes

Chocolate glaze

  1. Pour in a bowl the butter, chocolate powder, sugar and milk, then mix
  2. Take to the stove and continue mixing until it starts boiling
  3. Pour on top of the cake
