Chocolate powder brownie

Description

This sweet has the same delicious texture and flavor any brownie has, with its differential being the simple ingredients

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 1 1/2 cup of chocolate powder
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs with sugar for 1 minute
  2. Add the vanilla extract and the oil and beat a little more
  3. Add the chocolate powder and flour and beat until mixed
  4. Pour the mix into the baking dish greased with flour
  5. Take to the preheated oven at 220ÂºC for around 25-40 minutes
  6. To check if the brownie is ready, insert a toothpick and if it comes out with small pieces of dough on it, its ready
  7. Serve it warm or let it cool depending on your liking
